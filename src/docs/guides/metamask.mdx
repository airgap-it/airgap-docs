In this guide, we will explore how you can set up MetaMask as a watch-only wallet with AirGap Vault.

:::note MetaMask Mobile
If you interested in setting up MetaMask mobile please visit [here](/guides/metamask-mobile)
:::

MetaMask is a non-custodial wallet for accessing EVM-compatible blockchains.

If you already have MetaMask installed, you can skip the next step and proceed to pair MetaMask with AirGap Vault.

:::note Other EVM network
In this guide, we used the Ethereum Network as an example, but the AirGap Vault is blockchain agnostic. It can sign transactions on any EVM chain by adding the RPC of that chain to MetaMask. Nothing needs to be done on the Vault, besides the steps listed below.
We've tested it for BNBchain, Avalanche, Polygon, Celo, FUSE network, Moonriver, Moonbeam, and Astar.
:::

:::note Other EVM network
This guide is specific to MetaMask extension.
Please visit [here](https://support.airgap.it/guides/metamask-mobile) for the guide on how to connect AirGap to MetaMask mobile.
:::

:::Important
Before this, make sure you have turned on QR code intteraction on Vault. You can do it by going to Vault settings > vault interaction, and set it to qr code scanning.
:::

## 1. Installation

On your PC, visit the [MetaMask website](https://metamask.io/) and click on "Download" and follow the instructions for your browser.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage

</BrowserWindow>

Confirm the installation by clicking on "Add extension" from the popup.

Your browser will then download and automatically install the MetaMask extension.

You will get a confirmation popup after completing this step, after which your browser will automatically launch a new tab.

## 2. Pair AirGap Vault with MetaMask

Add a new ETH account to your Vault.

Click on the arrow sign then the plus + sign at the bottom right corner.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask1.png"} />

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask2.png"} />

Select ETH and click the create button. After that select, the newly created ETH account

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask3.png"} />

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask4.png"} />

Select the MetaMask button and Open MetaMask, Click on the circle at the top right corner and select connect hardware wallet.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask5.png"} />

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask6.png"} />

In MetaMask, click on the circle at the top right corner and select "Connect Hardware Wallet".

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/connect-hardware-wallet.png"
  alt="MetaMask Connect Hardware Wallet"
/>

</BrowserWindow>

Select "QR-based" and click "Continue".

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/select-qr-based-signer.png"
  alt="MetaMask Select QR Based Signer"
/>

</BrowserWindow>

Scan the QR-code in AirGap Vault.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/metamask-scan-qr-account.png"

## 3. Sending ETH Using MetaMask

In MetaMask, click "Send". Input the destination address and specify the amount.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/metamask-send-prepare.png"

</BrowserWindow>

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/transaction-confirm.png"
  alt="MetaMask Confirm Transaction"
/>

</BrowserWindow>

Open the Vault and scan the QR-code.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/metamask-request-signature.png"
  alt="MetaMask Request Signature"
/>

</BrowserWindow>

Inspect and verify the transaction details and ensure that they are correct. Then sign the transaction and select "offline device".

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask8.jpg"} />

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/metamask/metamask7.jpg"} />

Click the get signature button on MetaMask.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/get-signature.png"
  alt="MetaMask Get Signature"
/>

</BrowserWindow>

Scan the QR-code from the Vault.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://metamask">

<DynamicImage
  src="/img/metamask/metamask-scan-qr-signature.png"
  alt="MetaMask Scan Signature QR"
/>

After scanning the QR-code, the transaction will be sent to the Ethereum network and the process is completed. You can use the same process to send ERC-20 tokens or interact with any smart contract.
